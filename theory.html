Theory

Theory is the inward side of the work: the studio, the kilns, and the GENE project. These are the places where material understanding, process, reflection, and orientation gather before the work appears in Practice.

This side of the site is guided by the monks and shaped by the continuity of the studio story: place, matter, heat, method, and the larger ideas that hold them together.
