Bonsai Containers

Containers for living sculpture.

Bonsai containers are quiet partners in a larger composition. The container must support the tree without competing for attention. Form, weight, and surface are chosen to complement living material.

Bonsai Perspective

The low oval form and restrained copper surface allow the tree to remain visually dominant while providing a stable visual base. The container’s quiet turquoise glaze creates subtle movement without distracting from the bonsai composition.

Such containers are particularly well suited to informal upright and literati bonsai forms where restraint and balance are essential.
