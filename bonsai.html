Bonsai

Containers for living sculpture.

Bonsai containers are quiet partners in a larger composition. The vessel must support the tree without competing for attention.

These works explore balance and restraint — how clay can hold presence without asserting dominance.

Oval Bonsai Containers

The low oval forms and restrained surfaces allow the tree to remain visually dominant while providing a stable visual base.

Rectangle Bonsai Containers

The low rectangular form provides structure and grounding within the composition.

Round Bonsai Containers

The round form emphasizes balance and centered stillness.

Free Form Bonsai Containers

Free form containers explore gesture and irregularity while maintaining restraint.
