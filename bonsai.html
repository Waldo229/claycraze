Bonsai

Containers for living sculpture.

Bonsai containers are quiet partners in a larger composition. The vessel must support the tree without competing for attention. Form, weight, and surface are chosen to complement living material.

These works explore balance and restraint — how clay can hold presence without asserting dominance. Subtle shifts in surface and proportion allow the tree to remain visually primary while the container anchors the composition.
