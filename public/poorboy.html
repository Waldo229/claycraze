PoorBoy Kiln

Atmosphere, flame, and risk.

The PoorBoy kiln is an outdoor gas system — direct, exposed, and responsive. Unlike electric firing, it does not conceal its behavior. It must be watched, listened to, and felt as it moves.

Structure

A simple updraft system built for flexibility and immediacy. The kiln is not fixed in behavior — it responds to weather, pressure, and fuel conditions.

Firing Behavior

Flame movement, reduction, and atmosphere define the results. Timing is not just temperature — it is rhythm, adjustment, and response.

Observation

The kiln demands attention. Sound, flame color, and pressure shifts all register as signals. This is where GENE begins to participate — not as control, but as presence.
