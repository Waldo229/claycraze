Ikebana

Ikebana containers are shaped for line, interval, flower, and space. They ask for a different kind of quiet than bonsai pots: less ground, more relation.

I am interested in forms that support arrangement without becoming merely decorative—vessels that hold presence, asymmetry, and pause.
