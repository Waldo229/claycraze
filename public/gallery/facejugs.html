Face Jugs

Face jugs belong to an older, more unruly current in the work. They carry wit, character, exaggeration, and a kind of direct presence that asks to be met face to face.

I am interested in the point where humor, roughness, and personality become form—where the vessel is still a vessel, but also something more animated and confrontational.
