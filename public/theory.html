Theory

Theory is the inward side of the work: the studio, the kilns, materials, places, and the GENE project. These are the conditions under which understanding gathers before the work appears in Practice.

This side of the site concerns process, reflection, method, and orientation. It follows the continuity of the studio story: place, matter, heat, judgment, and the larger ideas that hold them together.
