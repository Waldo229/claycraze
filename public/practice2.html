Practice

Practice is where the work takes visible form: bonsai pots, ikebana containers, face jugs, sculpture, and other fired pieces that carry the studio’s thought into the world of use and looking.

If Theory gathers the inward conditions of the work, Practice is where those conditions become evidence. The objects shown here are not illustrations of ideas, but the things themselves—made, fired, handled, and sent out from the studio.
