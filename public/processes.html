Processes

This page gathers the working methods behind the making: forming, handling, surface, firing awareness, and revision.

Forming

Hand-building and wheel work each ask for different kinds of judgment. Form develops through repetition, touch, and proportion.

Surface

Slips, glazes, carving, and texture are not decoration alone. They are part of how the pot carries material character.

Revision

Process includes editing: what is kept, what is pared back, and what is abandoned in order to let the work become clearer.
