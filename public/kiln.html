Theory

Kilns

The kilns are the field of transformation: heat, time, atmosphere, and judgment. They carry the work from formed clay into its finished state.

This page gathers the two kiln lines in the studio: the Electric Skutt 1027 and the outdoor PoorBoy gas kiln.

Skutt 1027

The electric kiln used for controlled firing, measured pacing, and dependable studio work.

PoorBoy Kiln

The outdoor gas kiln: more exposed, more atmospheric, and connected to a different firing character.
