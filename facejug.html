Face Jugs

Expressive vessels with character and voice.

Face jugs carry a long tradition of expressive form, where surface, gesture, and construction combine to create presence and personality. Each piece develops through a direct engagement with the material, allowing features to emerge rather than be imposed.

These works explore character, asymmetry, and the tension between control and spontaneity — where the vessel becomes more than a container, taking on a life of its own.
