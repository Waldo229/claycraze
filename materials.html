Materials

Clay, glaze, slip, surface, and sourcing.

Clay

I work in high-temperature stoneware to produce bonsai containers that can better withstand the demands of nature and the elements. I use different clays for different purposes, sometimes including locally sourced “wild” clay from Virginia’s historic triangle for its color, texture, and unusual interaction with my glazes.

Glazes

I mix my own glazes and rely on simple, traditional, mostly iron-based or ash glazes.

Slips

I recommend Phil Rogers’ excellent book, Ash Glazes, as a sourcebook for both slips and glazes.
