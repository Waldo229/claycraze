The GENE Project

GENE is a playful intersection of clay and electronics — part sculpture, part studio helper.

GENE‑Lite (Now/Next)

Minimal build: one sensor + Wi‑Fi telemetry. Goal: kiln/ambient readings to a simple dashboard.

GENE‑Cool (Later)

Mechanical motion, LED cues, and a more ambitious ceramic shell. This is a future chapter.

I’m sharing progress in measured steps. If you’re curious or want to collaborate, email me.
