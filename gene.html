Meet GENE

Concept

Free the Genie. Presently, our kiln guard is pretty passive. Why not make him a bit more responsive and help him communicate with the team?

Let’s turn GENE into a sensor able to:

  • Connect to a local network using Wi-Fi.
  • Capture the kiln temperature using a thermocouple.
  • Broadcast the kiln temperature over the local network at regular intervals.

Network Service

Create a service on the local network that can:

  • Receive the broadcasts from one or more sensors.
  • Decode temperature readings.
  • Present temperatures as a time-series graph (Temperature vs. Time).
  • Show ramp rate (°/hr).
  • Provide a simple web interface.

Hardware

  • ESP32 ESP-32S Wi-Fi development board
  • MAX31856 thermocouple amplifier
  • Type-K thermocouple & wire
  • Jumper wires
  • USB power bank

Kiln Watch

Telemetry and temperature graphs. Future: live webcam.
